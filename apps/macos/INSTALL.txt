AURABOT INSTALLATION
====================

macOS 13+ Required

QUICK START:
------------
1. Drag AuraBot.app to your Applications folder

2. Right-click on AuraBot → Open
   (First time only - click "Open" on the warning)

3. Grant permissions when asked:
   - Screen Recording (for screenshots)
   - Accessibility (for Quick Enhance feature)

That's it!

TROUBLESHOOTING:
----------------

"App can't be opened" warning?
→ Right-click the app → Open → Open

Still having issues?
→ Open Terminal and run:
   xattr -cr /Applications/AuraBot.app

Need help?
→ Visit: https://github.com/yourusername/aurabot/issues

FEATURES:
---------
⌘⌥E - Quick Enhance selected text
Dashboard - View your captured memories
Chat - Ask about your activities
Settings - Configure capture interval

Enjoy AuraBot!
